Paradox of being a Rebel

May 20, 2019

All of my life I have been a rebel, not accepting things that were taught to me or said to me that didn't make the logic or convinced me.

Due to this nature, I was always isolated from society which has a tendency to follow the orthodox path or to be on a safer and secure path which according to me is not wrong but is a boring and non-creative approach towards life and made no purpose or logic to me.

This behavior to take risks and carefree attitude towards life was not convincing to my peers or anyone around me which created a careless image of mine in their minds. I never minded or cared for them to be convinced by my thoughts, but yet when your thought is outnumbered in society or peers,

you start to question yourself that ARE YOU GONNA MAKE IT? WHAT IF YOU FAIL?. You are not going to be consoled but rathered taunted by them who disagreed with you.
